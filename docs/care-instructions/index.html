Product Care

Care Instructions

Simple steps to keep your slippers clean, comfortable, and in great condition for everyday wear.

Clean with a Soft Cloth

Wipe slippers gently with a soft, slightly damp cloth after use.

Avoid Prolonged Direct Sunlight

Keep away from harsh sun to preserve colour and material integrity.

Store in a Cool Dry Place

Store in a ventilated area away from moisture when not in use.

Keep Away from Harsh Chemicals

Do not use bleach, solvents, or strong cleaning agents.

Allow to Air Dry Naturally

If wet, air dry at room temperature — avoid direct heat sources.

Avoid Machine Washing

Machine washing is not recommended unless specified for a product.
