Size Guide

Measure your foot, compare with our charts, and choose the size that fits best for everyday comfort.

How to Measure Your Foot

  1. Place a sheet of paper on a flat surface against a wall.
  2. Stand with your heel lightly touching the wall.
  3. Mark the tip of your longest toe on the paper.
  4. Measure the distance from the wall to the mark in centimetres.
  5. Compare your foot length with the size chart below.

Tips for Choosing the Right Size

  • •Measure feet in the evening when they are slightly larger.
  • •If between two sizes, choose the larger size for comfort.
  • •Wear the type of socks you plan to use with the slippers.
  • •Measure both feet and use the longer measurement.
  • •For kids, allow a little room for growth without oversizing.
